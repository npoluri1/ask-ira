# Apple Inc. (AAPL) — ESG Assessment Report
**Date:** 2025-02-20
**Analyst:** Priya Sharma
**ESG Score:** 7.8 / 10 (Above Average)

## Environmental (Score: 8.2 / 10, Weight: 30%)
- Carbon neutral for corporate operations since 2020; supply chain 2030 goal on track
- 100% of iPhone 17 packaging fiber-based, eliminating plastic
- Supplier clean energy program: 18GW installed, covering 85% of direct manufacturing
- Water replenishment: 7.5 billion gallons restored in high-stress watersheds

## Social (Score: 7.5 / 10, Weight: 30%)
- Supplier responsibility audits: 92% compliance rate, 120+ facilities audited annually
- Privacy positioning: strong differentiator, on-device processing, App Tracking Transparency
- Digital divide: expanded coding education to 500+ community colleges globally
- Worker safety: 45% reduction in injury rate across supply chain since 2020

## Governance (Score: 7.8 / 10, Weight: 40%)
- Board: 45%女性 diversity, strong independence (10/12 independent directors)
- Executive compensation: 80% performance-based, includes ESG modifier for NEO bonuses
- Tax: effective rate 14.5%, $10B+ cash taxes in US, ongoing OECD Pillar Two compliance
- Lobbying: disclosed all political contributions, $5.2M federal lobbying spend (transparent)

## Material ESG Issues
1. **Supply Chain Labor:** Foxconn/other suppliers face scrutiny over working conditions in China and India
2. **Right to Repair:** growing regulatory pressure to support independent repair; Apple's resistance creates reputational risk
3. **E-Waste:** despite recycling programs, only 24% of e-waste is properly recycled globally
4. **App Store Governance:** developer commission structure under regulatory scrutiny globally
