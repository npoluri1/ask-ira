# Apple Inc. (AAPL) — Investment Research Report
**Date:** 2025-03-15
**Analyst:** Shankar Cherukuri
**Rating:** BUY | **Target Price:** $265 | **Current Price:** $225

## Company Overview
Apple Inc. designs, manufactures, and markets smartphones (iPhone), personal computers (Mac), tablets (iPad), wearables (Apple Watch, AirPods), and services (App Store, Apple Music, iCloud, Apple TV+). With over 2 billion active devices, Apple has built one of the most valuable ecosystems in technology.

## Porter's Five Forces
- **Competitive Rivalry:** Moderate — premium smartphone competition from Samsung, Google Pixel. Laptop competition from Dell, HP, Lenovo.
- **Supplier Power:** Moderate-high — TSMC sole supplier for A/M-series chips. Apple uses scale to negotiate favorable terms.
- **Buyer Power:** Low — high switching costs due to ecosystem lock-in (iCloud, App Store purchases, AirDrop, iMessage).
- **Threat of Substitutes:** Moderate — Android offers comparable functionality, but lacks ecosystem integration.
- **Threat of New Entrants:** Low — brand equity, R&D scale, supply chain expertise create formidable barriers.

## Management Assessment
Tim Cook has demonstrated exceptional capital allocation since assuming CEO role in 2011. The transition from hardware-centric to services-driven revenue has improved margin structure and recurring revenue visibility. Aggressive share buybacks ($90B+ annually) are well-timed given the stock's valuation.

## Risk Factors
1. **Regulatory:** EU DMA investigations, US antitrust scrutiny of App Store practices
2. **Supply Chain:** Taiwan/China concentration risk (TSMC chips, Foxconn assembly)
3. **Competitive:** AI smartphone features from Samsung/Google could narrow differentiation
4. **Valuation:** 28.5x P/E is above 5-year average of 25x
