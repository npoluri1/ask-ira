# Alphabet Inc. (GOOGL) — Investment Research Report
**Date:** 2025-03-01
**Analyst:** Michael Chen
**Rating:** HOLD | **Target Price:** $195 | **Current Price:** $175

## Company Overview
Alphabet is a global technology conglomerate with dominant positions in search advertising, video (YouTube), cloud computing (GCP), and emerging technologies (Waymo, DeepMind). Google Search remains the primary profit engine, while Google Cloud recently achieved operating profitability. Other Bets represent optionality value with limited near-term revenue contribution.

## Key Catalysts & Concerns
**Catalysts:** Google Cloud profitability inflection, AI-driven Search improvements (SGE), aggressive share buybacks ($60B+), Waymo commercial expansion. **Concerns:** DOJ antitrust trial poses existential risk to search distribution agreements ($26B paid to Apple alone), AI search competing with traditional monetization, capital spending rising 50%+ for AI infrastructure.

## Valuation
- **DCF (Base Case):** $188
- **DCF (Antitrust Risk Case):** $145
- **SOTP:** Google Services $160 + Google Cloud $25 + Other Bets $10 = $195

## Risk Factors
1. **Antitrust:** DOJ remedy could force divestiture of Chrome/Android or end default search agreements
2. **AI Disruption:** ChatGPT and Perplexity eroding search moat; AI search has lower ad monetization
3. **Capex:** $50B+ annual capex for AI infrastructure pressuring FCF
4. **Regulatory:** EU Digital Markets Act compliance, privacy regulation impact on ad targeting
