# Microsoft Corporation (MSFT) — Investment Research Report
**Date:** 2025-03-10
**Analyst:** Shankar Cherukuri
**Rating:** BUY | **Target Price:** $480 | **Current Price:** $420

## Company Overview
Microsoft is the world's largest software company, operating across Productivity & Business Processes (Office, LinkedIn, Dynamics), Intelligent Cloud (Azure, server products, GitHub), and More Personal Computing (Windows, Surface, Xbox, Bing). The company has successfully transformed under Satya Nadella from a Windows-centric model to a cloud-first, AI-powered enterprise platform.

## Key AI Monetization Thesis
Microsoft is uniquely positioned to monetize AI through three vectors: (1) Azure AI Services — GPU compute and model-as-a-service with $65B annualized revenue run rate, (2) Microsoft 365 Copilot — $30/user/month premium, (3) GitHub Copilot — the most widely adopted AI developer tool with 1.8M+ paid subscribers.

## Risk Factors
1. **AI Competition:** Google, Amazon, and Meta investing heavily in competing AI infrastructure
2. **Regulatory:** FTC scrutiny of Activision Blizzard acquisition, EU DMA designation for certain products
3. **Execution:** Copilot enterprise adoption slower than expected
4. **Valuation:** 35x P/E reflects significant AI optimism; any monetization miss would compress multiple
